About Highland International

Highland International Pvt Ltd is private organization registered with Government of Nepal. It provides services for domestic and international telecom companies in business consultancy and Value added service. We are exclusive distributing partner of Vodafone, Qatar (www.vodafone.qa) in Nepal. We are also provider of value-added services of OTT to Nepal Telecom through our subsidiary OTV Solutions (otvsolution.com) and Win Nepal (www.wowtime.net). Win Nepal Pvt Ltd provides OTT services with 140 TV channels to Nepal Telecom (www.ntc.net.np) .
